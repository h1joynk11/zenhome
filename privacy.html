Zen Home

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zen Home ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. Zen Home is a meditation app designed with simplicity and privacy at its core.

Information We Do Not Collect

Zen Home does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services.
  • We do not collect usage data or behavioral data.
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • We do not serve advertisements.

All your meditation data (session history, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (In-App Purchase Processing)

Zen Home uses RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat may collect and process:

  • Transaction data (purchase receipt, product identifier, transaction ID)
  • Device identifiers necessary for purchase validation
  • Subscription/purchase status

This data is used solely for the purpose of processing and validating your purchase. RevenueCat's handling of this data is governed by their own privacy policy, available at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

App Store / Google Play

When you download or purchase Zen Home, Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) may collect information in accordance with their respective privacy policies. This is outside the scope of our control.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data includes:

  • Meditation session records
  • App settings and preferences
  • Purchase status

This data is not backed up to our servers (we do not operate any servers). Your device's built-in backup mechanisms (e.g., iCloud, Google Backup) may include this data as part of a full device backup.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all Zen Home data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app, or
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Zen Home does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we do not collect personal data from any user, no special provisions for children are necessary.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data (such as rights under GDPR or CCPA). Since Zen Home does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, these rights are inherently satisfied — there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, or delete.
