Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zen Home ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. Zen Home is a meditation app designed with simplicity and privacy at its core.

Information We Collect

Zen Home does not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information.

All your meditation data (session history, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience. This includes:

  • Screen views within the app (e.g., which onboarding screens are completed)
  • Feature usage events (e.g., session started, session completed)
  • App errors and crash reports

This data is anonymous — it cannot be used to identify you personally. We do not collect the content of your meditation sessions, personal notes, or any other personal information.

We do not serve advertisements, track your location, or use cookies.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (In-App Purchase Processing)

Zen Home uses RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat may collect and process:

  • Transaction data (purchase receipt, product identifier, transaction ID)
  • Device identifiers necessary for purchase validation
  • Subscription/purchase status

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

PostHog (Analytics)

Zen Home uses PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics. PostHog receives:

  • Anonymous event data (screen views, feature usage)
  • Device type and OS version
  • A randomly generated anonymous identifier

PostHog does not receive your name, email, location, or any meditation content. PostHog's privacy policy: https://posthog.com/privacy

Sentry (Error Monitoring)

Zen Home uses Sentry to monitor and fix app crashes. When an error occurs, Sentry may receive:

  • Error details and stack traces
  • Device type and OS version

Sentry does not receive any personal data or meditation content. Sentry's privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

App Store / Google Play

When you download or purchase Zen Home, Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) may collect information in accordance with their respective privacy policies. This is outside the scope of our control.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data includes:

  • Meditation session records
  • App settings and preferences
  • Purchase status

This data is not backed up to our servers (we do not operate any servers). Your device's built-in backup mechanisms (e.g., iCloud, Google Backup) may include this data as part of a full device backup.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all Zen Home data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app, or
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Zen Home does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we do not collect personal data from any user, no special provisions for children are necessary.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data (such as rights under GDPR or CCPA). Zen Home collects only anonymous usage data that cannot identify you. If you wish to opt out of analytics data collection, please contact us at the email below.
